# 🎟️ MeseoTicket – Paperless Museum Ticketing System

> 🚀 A smart, eco-friendly, and seamless digital ticketing solution designed to modernize museum visits.

---

## 🌟 Overview

**MeseoTicket** is a web-based application that eliminates traditional paper tickets and replaces them with a **fully digital, QR-based ticketing system**.

This project focuses on:
- Enhancing visitor experience 🎯  
- Reducing environmental impact 🌱  
- Improving operational efficiency 📊  

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Traditional ticketing systems face multiple challenges:

- ❌ Long queues and delays  
- ❌ Physical travel required to purchase tickets  
- ❌ Risk of contact-based transmission  
- ❌ Paper wastage and environmental impact  

---

## ✅ Solution

MeseoTicket introduces a **paperless, contactless ticketing platform** with:

- 📱 Digital ticket booking  
- 🔐 User authentication  
- 📊 Real-time ticket storage using Firebase  
- 📷 QR code-based entry system  
- ⚡ Fast and seamless user experience

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User logs in 🔐  
2. Selects museum & booking slot 🏛️  
3. Enters visitor details 👥  
4. Ticket is stored in Firebase ☁️  
5. QR code is generated 📷  
6. Scan QR at entry for verification ✅

Indian Museum

Established in 1814, the Indian Museum in Kolkata is one of the oldest and largest museums in India. It houses a vast collection of artifacts, including art, archaeology, anthropology, and geology.

Location



National Museum

The National Museum in New Delhi is one of the premier museums in India. It has an extensive collection of art and artifacts, representing different periods of Indian history and culture. The museum's exhibits include sculptures, paintings, manuscripts, and more.

Location



Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya

Located in Mumbai, this museum was established in the early 20th century. It features a diverse range of exhibits, including art, artifacts, and decorative pieces from various regions and time periods.

Location



Salar Jung Museum

The Salar Jung Museum is one of the three national museums in India and is renowned for its collection of art and antiques. It houses a diverse range of items, including paintings, sculptures, textiles, manuscripts, and furniture.

Location



Victoria Memorial

While not a traditional museum, the Victoria Memorial in Kolkata is an iconic structure that houses a museum within its premises. Built in memory of Queen Victoria, the museum showcases artifacts from the British colonial period.
